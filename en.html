Join us!

You'll have a mentor available for you
By joining BEST Lisbon, you will have the opportunity to have a BESTie (person from BEST) that will help you giving your first steps inside BEST Lisbon and BEST International.
You can organize new events
In BEST Lisbon, each event has a team (called Core Team) responsible for each event. By joining us, you can join one of these teams and create bounds with the team that will last a lifetime. Some of our events are: EBEC (European BEST Engineering Competition) and BIV (BEST Inside View).
You can try different areas and see which one is more suitable for you!
BEST Lisbon has 4 departments (CR - Corporate Relations, IT - Information Technology, PR - Public Relations, Design) and 3 teams (Human Resources team, Grants team and and External Relations team). With BEST Lisbom, you can try these teams/departments and decide which one you like more.
You can develop new skills and create friendships for the eternity
BEST is the ideal place for you to develop your leadership skills, time management skills and communication skills and create friendships that will last a lifetime!
